Before You Book — Good to Know

Please read through these important booking details before submitting your request.

📅
How Far in Advance Should I Book?
▼

Our dates fill up fast — especially Saturdays and Sundays at 2 PM and 5 PM. If those time slots are on your radar, book as early as you know you want a party.

Outside of peak times, 3–4 weeks is a fair lead time. 5–6 weeks is safer.

💡 Use our scheduling system to check availability and reserve your event. Have questions first? Call or text us and we'll get back to you quickly.
⚠️ Important: It's possible we may ask for a small adjustment to your selected time to ensure we can arrive on time or reach the next event on schedule. Do not send out invitations before we have confirmed your event and you have secured your deposit.
🔍
No Slot Open for Your Desired Day?
▼

Our automatic calendar doesn't always show every available option — we can often see openings in our system that aren't visible online.

If you're looking for a particular day (or need some flexibility), contact us directly and we'll double-check or offer alternatives if they exist.

Weekday Event Times Are More Flexible
▼

Can't find the exact time you want on a weekday? Choose the closest available time and tell us your preferred time in the booking notes field above.

Weekends are much busier and tend to be more rigid — if you need a specific weekend time, book as early as possible.
