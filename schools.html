📋 Before You Book

Good To Know Before You Reserve

We get these questions a lot — so we put the answers right here. Read through before reserving your date and you'll be all set.

Certain spots go quickly — Saturday and Sunday at 2 PM and 5 PM are our most requested time slots. Once you know you want an event, don't wait on those. Reserve it.

For everything else, giving us 3–4 weeks notice is usually enough. 5–6 weeks gives you more breathing room and the best shot at your first choice.

💡 Our online booking system makes it easy to browse availability and grab your spot. If you'd rather talk it through first, reach out — we respond fast.
⚠️ Heads up: We occasionally need to shift your start time slightly to keep our schedule running smoothly. It happens rarely, but it can. Hold off on sending invitations until we've confirmed your event in writing and your deposit is in.

The calendar on our site shows most availability — but not all of it. There are slots we can see on the backend that don't always show up online.

Before writing off a date, give us a call or shoot us a message. If there's any way to make it work, we'll find it. We've pulled off same-week bookings before.
